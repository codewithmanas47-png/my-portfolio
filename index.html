🍕 Food Delivery
Food Delivery Platform — Late Orders to Real-time Tracking
❌ Late Deliveries React Node.js Socket.io MongoDB ✅ On-time
❌ Before — No Tracking, Late Orders
✦ After — Fast Ordering + Live Tracking
🔴 Challenge

The food delivery website had a broken ordering system — payments failed with Error 408, no real-time tracking existed, and customers had no way to know where their food was. Orders were arriving 2–3 hours late with zero communication. 65% of customers were abandoning orders mid-checkout, and negative reviews were destroying the brand. Monthly losses exceeded Rs. 200,000.

🔵 Solution

I rebuilt the entire platform — beautiful food menu with real photos and ratings, fast checkout with Easypaisa/JazzCash and card payments, real-time live order tracking powered by Socket.io (customers see exactly where their rider is), automated SMS updates at every stage, and a rider management dashboard. Food now arrives on time and customers can track every step from home.

Backend Transformation
Before — Typical Client Codebase

app.get('/users', (req,res) => {

  res.json(all) // exposed everything
})
// Passwords stored in plain text
// No rate limiting → DDoS risk

After — My Production Standard
// Secured, rate-limited, typed
router.get('/users/:id', [
  verifyJWT, checkRole('admin'),
  rateLimiter, validateParams
], async (req, res) => {
  const user = await User.findById(req.params.id)
  if (!user) throw new NotFoundError()
  res.json({ data: sanitize(user) })
})
